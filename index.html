Skills

HTML5 is the foundation of a website. Having proper knowledge about HTML is very useful for creating websites. I love finding new ways to use HTML to my advantage and design the outcome I want.

CSS3 is my favourite part of designing a website because it allows you to use your creativity. There are endless uses for CSS and it can be made even easier by using plug-ins like bootstrap.

Javascript is used for making a webpage interactive and dynamic. I love Javascript for its ability to do anything that you want, it is the language with the most possibilities. I can also write in jQuery.

Behind the scenes of a website may lie some heavy coding which can come in the form of a Python function. Creating functions and finding different ways to create loops and programs can be very amusing.

Databases are really fun to use! creating complex queries is really interesting for me and I love finding new ways to use sub-queries and other features of database querying. I use MySQL workbench and have expirience with the world database and the flight database.

Creating responsive webpage art using pictures is difficult, but using vector images is a lot easier. I use a library called Raphael.js which creates vector images as DOM objects, so you can attach JavaScript event handlers and edit them at any time.

Photoshop is by far the best tool for editing photos, you can manipulate images in any way you want. I've been using photoshop for over 7 years and have some examples of my work below.

I have been editing videos with Premiere Pro for the past 7 years, along with Adobe After Effects, creating YouTube videos and other content. My uncle works at Adobe and I'm lucky enough that he's gotten me a copy of both programs for so many years.

About Me

My name is Shawn Beaton. I grew up in Greely, a small village outside of the Capital of Canada. I was always a happy kid, I loved attention and loved making others happy.

When I turned 5 I began playing hockey and fell in love with the greatest sport on Earth. I played defence growing up and was good enough to make it onto competitive teams. I won two championships with the Gloucester Rangers and played a total of 5 years for them. Now I play juvenile for the Metcalfe Jets and love watching NHL Hockey. My favourite team is the Ottawa Senators but I also like the Toronto Maple Leafs and the Tampa Bay Lightning.

I graduated in 2017 from Osgoode Township Highschool. It was a small town and everyone knew everyone at that school. I had a lot of fun during my 4 years.

A year and 8 months ago I fell in love with the most beautiful girl who would change my life forever. Her name is Meghann, she is my everything, the one I think about all day long and the one who keeps my sanity in check. She would do anything for me and I would do anything for her.

Fishing is my favourite pastime. It's so much fun to do. There's no better feeling than catching a really heavy, big fish.

Growing up playing hockey, a lot of my buddies played lacrosse, but I didn't. I was always interested but never tried it until highschool in grade 11. From my first game I played I fell in love with the sport. I played with my highschool team to win the city championship twice. Now I play in a summer league and have a lot of good times.

This is a picture of Meg and I on our first roadtrip to Toronto. We went to the CN Tower, Ripley's Aquarium, and Wonderland.
